Purple Non-Sulfur Bacterium

Abbreviated as "PNS"

Visit PubMed for more PNS information!

Characteristics:

  1. PNS play an important role in wastewater treatment, bioremediation, and the production of biogas and hydrogen.
  2. Use organic compounds as electron donors for photosynthesis.
  3. PNSB thrive in a variety of anaerobic to microaerophilic environments.
  4. Exhibit a distinctive purple color
  5. Their ecological role is significant, contributing to the carbon cycle and nitrogen fixation in some species.
